Declaration by Owner/Occupier

The process to submit the declaration has following steps:
  • Create a new declaration by clicking Create Declaration button below
  • Fill the Form K
  • Click Submit button on the final screen
Once a declaration in created by clicking on Create Declaration on this screen, it is saved in our system and you can resume filling it if you leave it incomplete by accessing the declaration from My Applications link on the side bar. The declaration is only submitted to the department for review once you click Submit on the final screen.
